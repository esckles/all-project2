Scrum


The scrum template is pre-formatted to help teams organize and plan their
work within the Scrum framework. Scrum is one of the most popular
frameworks for implementing agile. With scrum, the product is built in a
series of fixed-length iterations called sprints that give teams a framework
for shipping on a regular cadence.
