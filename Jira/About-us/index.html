what is verge

Verge is a leading provider of tech updates, news, and educational resources. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with the
knowledge and skills to succeed in a rapidly changing tech landscape. we beleive that technology should be accessible to everyone,
and we are committed to make it easy for anyone to stay updated and learn new skills
